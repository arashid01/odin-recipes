Dill Pickle Chicken Skewers

Description

This quick and flavorful dish features bite-sized pieces of boneless, skinless chicken breast cooked in a sweet and savory glaze made from honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes. The result is a tender, golden-brown chicken with a delightful balance of sweetness and a hint of heat. Perfectly paired with steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions, it's a simple yet satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
